Vietnamese Style Baked Chicken

A Savory and Caramelized Vietnamese Favorite

About Dish Ingredients Instructions

Vietnamese Style Baked Chicken, also known as Gà Nướng, is a flavorful dish made with garlic, fish sauce, honey, and soy sauce. Baked until golden and caramelized, it balances savory, sweet, and umami flavors and is traditionally served with rice, fresh herbs, and pickled vegetables.

About the Dish

Gà Nướng is a popular Vietnamese chicken dish traditionally grilled over charcoal. This oven-baked version preserves authentic flavor while making it accessible for home kitchens. The marinade tenderizes the chicken and creates a caramelized outer layer.

Ingredients

  • Bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
  • Garlic
  • Fish sauce
  • Soy sauce
  • Oyster sauce
  • Honey
  • Brown sugar
  • Vegetable oil
  • Black pepper
  • Paprika
  • Chili flakes
  • Lemongrass

Instructions

Preparing the Marinade

Mix garlic, fish sauce, soy sauce, oyster sauce, honey, sugar, oil, and pepper.

Marinating

Coat chicken thoroughly and refrigerate for at least one hour (overnight preferred).

Baking

Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Bake for 35–45 minutes until internal temperature reaches 165°F.

Finishing

Broil briefly for caramelization and garnish with fresh herbs.
